About Nikhil Sahai

A Visionary in Data and Entrepreneurship

Nikhil Sahai is a seasoned data analytics leader with over 15 years of experience in leveraging data to drive business growth, efficiency, and innovation. With a Master’s degree in Business Analytics from Singapore Management University, Nikhil has built a distinguished career working across diverse industries—including media-streaming, e-commerce, retail, healthcare, and transport.

Over the past decade, he has specialized in setting up data systems and infrastructures from scratch, particularly in high-growth environments. Nikhil has led the development of robust data ecosystems for renowned companies in the e-commerce and media sectors. From designing data pipelines and warehouses to creating actionable dashboards and predictive analytics, he has played a pivotal role in transforming raw data into meaningful insights that empower decision-making.

As the Regional Lead for Data Insights and Analytics at a major media conglomerate, Nikhil successfully launched data strategies across Asia-Pacific, delivering measurable improvements in user engagement, retention, and operational efficiency. His hands-on expertise includes designing scalable solutions for both startups and enterprises alike, ensuring that data becomes a core asset driving long-term success.

Nikhil’s ability to translate complex data concepts into practical applications has made him a trusted advisor to senior executives and startup founders. His work spans data engineering, business intelligence, machine learning, and storytelling with data—equipping organizations to unlock the true potential of their information.

An accomplished public speaker, Nikhil has shared his insights on data management and analytics at several high-profile industry conferences. His innovative contributions have earned him numerous accolades for delivering impactful, data-backed results.

When he’s not solving data challenges, Nikhil shares his knowledge through blogs on his website, experiments with emerging technologies like home automation, and participates in marathons and triathlons. He believes that data is not just about numbers—it’s a narrative that every business needs to craft for its growth.

In Data for Entrepreneurs: Basic Data Setup Guide, Nikhil combines his wealth of experience and passion for data to guide startup founders, tech leads, and business managers in building strong data foundations. With this book, he demystifies the process of setting up data infrastructure, offering practical strategies and actionable advice to empower young companies to thrive in today’s data-driven world.
